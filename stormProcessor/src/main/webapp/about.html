About

Storm is an internal Rule Financial Java-based project developed by group of four graduates. The idea is to create web application computing financial indicators (simple Moving Average and Exponential Moving Average) and drawing chart for it in real time based on intraday stock data from NYSE market.



Trade use

Exponential Moving average is together with its simple counterpart (MA) considered to be one of the most common Indicators in nearly any technical analysis software available in the market today. Itâ€™s a trend following indicator. Moving averages can be used to identify a trend by using the slope of the average (or lack of slope in a ranging market). They can also be used in trend trading systems to enter and exit trades by waiting for price and moving average crossovers, or for multiple moving average crossovers. Moving averages are also used in the calculations of many other indicators, such as the Moving Average Convergence Divergence (MACD), and the Commodity Channel Index (CCI).
